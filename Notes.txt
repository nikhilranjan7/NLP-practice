## Tokenizer : Are of two types,
               word Tokenizer -- separates by words
               sentence Tokenizer -- separated by sentence

## Corpora : Body of text ,eg, medical journal, English language, (basically anything revolving around the same topic)

## Lexicon : word and their meaning, jargon specific meaning, or the meaning related to the context(corpora)

## stop words : words we don't care about, eg., a, an, the

## Stemming : To derive a root word from the various form of the same word, eg, riding -- rid

## Chunking : Using regular expression in pos tagging to find any specific tag and word.

## Chinking : Removal of anyextra thing from chunk

## Lemmatizing : Same as stemming but the root word has some meaning and similar to the original word
